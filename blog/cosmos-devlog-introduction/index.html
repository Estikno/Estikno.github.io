Introduction

Embarking on a new project is always an exciting journey, and this time around, I’ve set my sights on creating a chess engine that doubles up as a game review app. The primary goal? To provide a learning tool for chess enthusiasts, helping them analyze and improve their games.

I know I should not start a new project withouth finishing remaining ones. But I think I have lot to learn about. And do not worry, as I would also continue with the other projects.

Game Engine

To bring this vision to life, I’ve opted to harness the power of Unity as the game engine for this project. Unity’s versatility and user-friendly interface make it an ideal choice, ensuring a smooth development process as I dive into the intricacies of chess mechanics.

Current Progress

As I venture into this project, the foundations are taking shape. The focus is currently on crafting a basic yet intuitive user interface, ensuring that users can seamlessly navigate through the app. Simultaneously, I’m meticulously refining the game mechanics to guarantee an authentic chess-playing experience.

What to Expect

Given the demands of school, progress updates might not flood in as frequently as I’d like. However, rest assured, each step forward is a step toward creating a valuable learning tool for chess aficionados. The primary aim is to offer a platform that not only entertains but educates.

As an added bonus, there will be a small artificial intelligence component, allowing users to test their skills against the app. While the AI won’t be a grandmaster, it will serve as a worthy opponent for those seeking a challenging game.

Stay Tuned

So, fellow chess enthusiasts, stay tuned for updates on this exciting venture. While the journey may be challenging, the destination promises to be a space where learning meets leisure. The game review app is on the horizon, and your feedback and insights will be crucial in shaping its evolution.

Let the game begin!
